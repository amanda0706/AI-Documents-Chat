This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Acme Solutions Ltd
("Provider") and Globex Corp ("Customer") as of 1 January 2026.

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
Provider agrees to deliver software development, consulting, and support services as
described in Statements of Work ("SOW") issued under this Agreement. Each SOW shall
specify deliverables, timelines, and applicable fees. Provider may subcontract portions
of the work with prior written approval from Customer.

2. TERM
This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues for a period of two (2)
years unless earlier terminated in accordance with Section 9. It will automatically renew
for successive one-year terms unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal
at least sixty (60) days before the end of the then-current term.

3. FEES AND INVOICING
Customer shall pay Provider the fees set out in the applicable SOW. Provider shall issue
invoices on a monthly basis. All invoices are payable within sixty (60) days of the
invoice date. Amounts not paid within sixty (60) days shall accrue interest at the rate
of 1.5% per month on the outstanding balance.

4. PAYMENT TERMS
Customer shall pay all fees within sixty (60) days of the invoice date. Provider reserves
the right to suspend services if any invoice remains unpaid for more than thirty (30)
days after becoming due, provided that Provider gives Customer ten (10) days written
notice before such suspension.

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All work product, inventions, and deliverables created by Provider exclusively for
Customer under a SOW ("Custom Deliverables") shall, upon full payment, be assigned to
Customer. All pre-existing intellectual property and general tools or methodologies
developed independently by Provider remain the sole property of Provider. Provider
grants Customer a non-exclusive, non-transferable licence to use Provider's background
IP solely as embedded in the Custom Deliverables.

6. CONFIDENTIALITY
Each party ("Receiving Party") agrees to hold in strict confidence all Confidential
Information disclosed by the other party ("Disclosing Party") and not to disclose it to
any third party without prior written consent. Confidential Information excludes
information that: (a) is or becomes publicly known through no breach of this Agreement;
(b) was rightfully known to the Receiving Party before disclosure; or (c) is
independently developed by the Receiving Party. This obligation survives termination of
the Agreement for a period of five (5) years.

7. DATA PROTECTION
Each party shall comply with applicable data protection legislation. Customer is the
data controller; Provider is the data processor in respect of any personal data
processed on Customer's behalf. Provider shall implement appropriate technical and
organisational measures to protect personal data and shall notify Customer of any
personal data breach within seventy-two (72) hours of becoming aware of it.

8. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
Neither party shall be liable for any indirect, incidental, special, consequential, or
punitive damages arising out of or related to this Agreement, even if the other party
has been advised of the possibility of such damages. Each party's total aggregate
liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the fees paid or payable by Customer in
the twelve (12) months immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim.

9. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement for convenience by giving the other party
ninety (90) days prior written notice. Either party may terminate immediately upon
written notice if the other party: (a) commits a material breach and fails to remedy it
within thirty (30) days of receiving written notice of the breach; (b) becomes insolvent
or makes an assignment for the benefit of creditors; or (c) ceases to carry on business.

10. AUTOMATIC RENEWAL
This Agreement and each active SOW will automatically renew unless the non-renewing
party provides written notice of its intent not to renew at least sixty (60) days before
the expiry of the then-current term. Customer is responsible for tracking renewal dates.

11. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement is governed by the laws of England and Wales. The parties submit to the
exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of England and Wales for the resolution of any
dispute arising under or in connection with this Agreement.

12. GENERAL
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties in relation to its
subject matter and supersedes all prior representations, negotiations, or arrangements.
No variation to this Agreement is effective unless made in writing and signed by both
parties. If any provision of this Agreement is found to be invalid or unenforceable, the
remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.
