This Supplier Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Northwind Components Ltd
("Supplier") and Globex Corp ("Buyer") as of 15 February 2026.

1. SUPPLY OF GOODS AND SERVICES
Supplier agrees to supply the goods and ancillary services described in each Purchase
Order issued by Buyer under this Agreement. Supplier shall fulfil orders within the
lead times stated in each Purchase Order. Supplier shall not sub-contract fulfilment
without Buyer's prior written consent.

2. TERM
This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues for one (1) year unless
earlier terminated under Section 8. Either party may renew for a further year by
providing written notice no fewer than thirty (30) days before expiry.

3. PRICING AND PAYMENT
Buyer shall pay Supplier the unit prices agreed in the applicable Purchase Order.
Supplier shall issue invoices upon delivery. Payment is due within thirty (30) days
of the invoice date. Invoices paid after thirty (30) days shall not accrue interest
unless Supplier provides fourteen (14) days written notice of intent to charge.

4. DELIVERY AND ACCEPTANCE
All goods are delivered DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) to Buyer's nominated address. Buyer
has ten (10) business days after delivery to inspect and reject non-conforming goods.
Accepted goods may not subsequently be returned except for latent defects discovered
within six (6) months of delivery.

5. WARRANTIES
Supplier warrants that all goods supplied are: (a) free from defects in materials and
workmanship for a period of twelve (12) months from delivery; (b) compliant with all
applicable laws and regulations; and (c) do not infringe any third-party intellectual
property rights.

6. CONFIDENTIALITY
Each party shall keep confidential all proprietary information received from the other
party and shall not disclose it to any third party without prior written consent.
Confidential information does not include information that is publicly available or
independently developed. This obligation continues for three (3) years after
termination of this Agreement.

7. LIABILITY
Neither party excludes liability for death or personal injury caused by negligence, or
for fraud or fraudulent misrepresentation. Subject to the foregoing, neither party is
liable for indirect or consequential losses. Each party's aggregate liability is capped
at the total fees paid under this Agreement in the six (6) months preceding the claim.
The liability cap shall not apply to fraud, wilful misconduct, or breach of
confidentiality obligations.

8. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement by giving thirty (30) days written notice.
Either party may terminate immediately if the other party commits a material breach
that is not remedied within fourteen (14) days of written notice, or if the other
party becomes insolvent.

9. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement is governed by the laws of Scotland. Disputes shall be submitted first
to senior management of both parties for good-faith resolution before any legal
proceedings are commenced.

10. GENERAL
This Agreement supersedes all prior agreements between the parties on the same subject
matter. Any amendment must be in writing and signed by authorised representatives of
both parties.
