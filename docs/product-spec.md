# Product specification

## Product

ClausePilot is an AI Contract / Document Assistant for people who need to understand contracts quickly, compare revisions, and surface risky clauses before approval.

## Primary users

- freelancers,
- small businesses,
- procurement or operations teams,
- anyone reviewing repeatable business agreements.

## Core job to be done

> "Help me understand what matters in this contract before I sign or send it."

## Main workflows

1. Upload a contract
2. Read summary and risk score
3. Ask targeted questions
4. Search supporting fragments
5. Compare with another version
6. Share findings with a collaborator

## Why this is useful

- reduces review time,
- makes hidden clauses easier to notice,
- gives non-lawyers a clearer first pass,
- creates a strong bridge between document search and decision support.

## Non-goals for the local version

- legal advice,
- final legal approval,
- perfect semantic understanding,
- cloud-scale collaboration.
